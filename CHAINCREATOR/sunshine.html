About Us:


Sunshine Worldwide School is the brain child of Deepak Khaitan founded in June 2003. It was conceptualised in December 2002, with a vision to foster real-life education, steeped in the values of a traditional Indian outlook, assuring a balance of international and ethnic expectations.
It is a school with a happy, supportive atmosphere which follows an evolved system of education. An exciting curriculum (Over 13 year researched curriculum) is combined with a full programme outside the classroom. The use of efficient modern technology, further facilitates and enhances a 'Child-centric' approach. Students are groomed into confident, responsible, talented and complete individuals.
In all its programme, Sunshine Worldwide School seeks to discover and nurture the special gifts each student possesses, to deepen each student's understanding of the complexities of the world and to inspire each to his or her full potential.
Our aim is to nurture our children to follow their own path, paved by their individual dreams and to shape their future with a unique positive attitude.
Our Mission: To provide the opportunity of choice alongside the freedom to realize ones’ ultimate potential.
10 Core principals of YES Learning (USPs):
  • First and foremost, unconditional happiness.
  • A positive approach to everything, without exception.
  • Freedom without fear or licence.
  • Stress free, non-competitive environment.
  • Experiential learning: activity based curriculum.
  • Cognitive learning: inculcating creativity and logical thinking.
